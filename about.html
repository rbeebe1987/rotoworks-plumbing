Our Story

RotoWorks Plumbing is a locally owned company serving the Temecula Valley. We know the plumbing issues that come with living out here — hard water that eats through fixtures, aging sewer lines in the older parts of Temecula and Murrieta, and the clay soil that shifts and cracks pipes over time.

We're a small operation and we like it that way. Every technician is licensed and background-checked. We show up on time, explain the problem in plain English, and give you a price before we start — no surprises when the invoice comes.

Most of our work comes from repeat customers and referrals. We'd rather do a good job and earn your next call than upsell you on something you don't need.
