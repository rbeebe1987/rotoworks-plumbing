Plumbing Across Riverside County

RotoWorks Plumbing is based in the heart of the Temecula Valley, and from that central location we serve a wide swath of southwestern Riverside County. If you're outside of Temecula, Murrieta, or Menifee but still within our service radius, this page is for you. We regularly take calls from homeowners in Lake Elsinore, Wildomar, Canyon Lake, Hemet, Perris, Winchester, French Valley, and unincorporated Sun City.

Lake Elsinore sits along the I-15 corridor about 20 minutes north of our base, and it's one of the fastest-growing communities in the region. The newer developments near Tuscany Hills and Rosetta Canyon feature modern plumbing systems, but many older lakeside homes have aging infrastructure that needs attention — especially sewer laterals affected by root intrusion from the mature trees along the lakeshore. Wildomar, just south of Lake Elsinore, was only incorporated in 2008 and has a mix of rural properties on well water and newer subdivisions on municipal supply, each with distinct plumbing needs.

Canyon Lake is a gated community built around a private lake, and its homes range from 1960s originals to recent custom builds. The community's hilly terrain can make sewer line work challenging, and that's an area where our camera inspection and trenchless repair capabilities really shine. Further east, Hemet and Perris have some of the most affordable housing in the county, but many of those homes have deferred maintenance on plumbing systems that were already dated when the current owners moved in. Whether it's a full repipe, a water heater swap, or an emergency at 3 AM, RotoWorks gets the job done right throughout Riverside County.
