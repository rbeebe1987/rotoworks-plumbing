Your Trusted Plumber in Menifee

Menifee is one of the fastest-growing cities in California, and that growth brings a unique mix of plumbing challenges. RotoWorks Plumbing works in Menifee every week, and we understand the difference between a clogged drain in a brand-new Audie Murphy Ranch home and a corroded main line under a 1970s ranch house in Sun City.

The Sun City area is where many of Menifee's oldest homes are located. These properties were built as part of a retirement community decades ago, and their plumbing systems reflect that era — galvanized steel water lines that restrict flow, original cast iron drain stacks, and water heaters that have been replaced multiple times over the years. If you're buying or renovating in Sun City, a plumbing inspection from RotoWorks can save you from expensive surprises. On the other end of the spectrum, Audie Murphy Ranch features homes built after 2015 with modern PEX piping, but even new homes aren't immune to issues like improper grading on drain lines or builder-grade fixtures that fail prematurely.

We serve all three Menifee zip codes: 92584, 92585, and 92586. Whether you're near Heritage Lake, shopping at Countryside Marketplace, or tucked into one of the neighborhoods around Menifee Lakes, RotoWorks is always a short drive away. The area's hot summers put extra demand on water heaters and irrigation systems, and our team is equipped to handle both residential and light commercial plumbing throughout the city.
