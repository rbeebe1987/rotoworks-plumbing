Your Temecula-Based Plumber for North County San Diego

Most people don't realize that Temecula sits right on the Riverside-San Diego county line. That means RotoWorks Plumbing is just as close to Fallbrook and Bonsall as we are to Murrieta. We've been serving North San Diego County homeowners for years, and many of our regular customers are on the San Diego side of the border.

Fallbrook — known as the "Avocado Capital of the World" — is only about 20 minutes from our base. Many Fallbrook properties are on larger rural lots with well water systems and septic tanks, which require a plumber who understands the difference between municipal and private water infrastructure. The community of Rainbow, tucked between Fallbrook and Temecula along the I-15, is another area we serve frequently. Bonsall, just south of Fallbrook, has a similar mix of rural estates and equestrian properties where plumbing runs can be long and complex.

Further south into North County, we regularly work in Vista, Oceanside, San Marcos, and Escondido. Vista's older neighborhoods near downtown have homes from the 1950s-70s with original plumbing that's reaching the end of its useful life. Oceanside's coastal location means salt air accelerates corrosion on outdoor fixtures and water heater components. Valley Center and Poway round out our North County coverage, with Valley Center's rural properties often presenting the same well-and-septic challenges we see in Fallbrook. Whether you need a quick repair or a major project, RotoWorks delivers the same honest, affordable service across the county line.
