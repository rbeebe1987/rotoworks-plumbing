Sewer line problems are nobody's idea of fun

A sewer line problem is one of the most serious plumbing issues a homeowner can face. When your main sewer line is blocked, damaged, or compromised by tree roots, every drain in your home is affected — toilets back up, showers drain slowly, and in the worst cases, raw sewage can surface in your yard or back up into your home. RotoWorks Plumbing provides complete sewer line cleaning, inspection, repair, and replacement services throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee, using professional-grade equipment to resolve even the most stubborn sewer problems.

Our sewer line services begin with a video camera inspection — a small, waterproof camera is fed through your sewer line to give us a real-time view of the pipe's interior condition. This allows us to identify the exact location and nature of the problem, whether it's a grease buildup, tree root intrusion, a collapsed pipe section, an offset joint, or a buildup of scale and debris over decades of use. For blockages and root intrusion, we use high-pressure hydro-jetting, which blasts water at up to 4,000 PSI to scour the inside of the pipe clean and cut through tree roots. Unlike mechanical snaking, which only punches a hole through the blockage, hydro-jetting restores the full diameter of the pipe and significantly extends the time between cleanings.

When the camera reveals damage that cleaning alone can't fix — cracked pipes, bellied sections, or severe root damage — we offer both traditional excavation repair and trenchless pipe lining depending on the situation. Trenchless methods can often reline your existing sewer pipe without digging up your yard, driveway, or landscaping, saving you time and restoration costs. We present all options with clear pricing and honest recommendations so you can make the best decision for your property. Every sewer line service is backed by our price beat guarantee.
