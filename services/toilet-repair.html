Toilet running, leaking, or won't flush?

A malfunctioning toilet is one of the most disruptive plumbing problems in any household. Whether it's running nonstop, won't flush properly, rocks on its base, or leaks onto the bathroom floor, a toilet issue demands prompt attention. RotoWorks Plumbing provides fast, professional toilet repair and replacement services throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee. We carry common replacement parts on our trucks, which means most repairs are completed in a single visit without the need for return trips or special orders.

The most common toilet problems we see include a toilet that runs continuously — usually caused by a worn flapper valve or a faulty fill valve — persistent clogs that a plunger can't clear, leaking at the base due to a deteriorated wax ring, phantom flushing caused by a slow leak from the tank into the bowl, and weak or incomplete flushing from mineral deposits or an outdated design. While some of these issues seem minor, a running toilet can waste over 200 gallons of water per day, significantly inflating your monthly bill. Our technicians diagnose the root cause quickly and explain the most cost-effective fix before any work begins.

If your toilet is beyond repair — cracked porcelain, repeated internal failures, or simply outdated — we handle full replacements as well. We can help you choose a modern, water-efficient model that meets California's water conservation standards while providing powerful, reliable flushing performance. Installation includes removing the old unit, inspecting the flange and drain connection, setting the new toilet with a fresh wax seal, and testing everything thoroughly. All work is backed by our price beat guarantee and honest upfront pricing.
