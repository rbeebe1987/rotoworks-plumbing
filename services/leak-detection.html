Think you might have a leak?

Water leaks are one of the most damaging plumbing problems a homeowner can face, and the worst part is that many leaks remain hidden for weeks or even months before the damage becomes visible. A slab leak beneath your foundation, a pinhole leak inside a wall, or a slow drip under your crawl space can lead to mold growth, structural damage, and water bills that skyrocket without explanation. RotoWorks Plumbing uses advanced detection technology to locate leaks quickly and accurately throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee — without tearing up your property in the process.

Our leak detection methods include electronic listening devices that amplify the sound of water escaping from pipes, thermal imaging cameras that reveal temperature differences caused by moisture behind walls and under floors, and pressure testing to isolate the exact section of pipe where the leak is occurring. For slab leaks — a common issue in Southern California homes built on concrete foundations — we use specialized equipment to pinpoint the leak's location within inches, minimizing the amount of concrete that needs to be accessed for repair. Early detection can save you thousands of dollars in water damage restoration and prevent the health hazards associated with mold and mildew.

Once we've located the leak, we present your repair options with clear, upfront pricing. Solutions range from spot repairs and pipe section replacement to full repiping for older homes with deteriorating copper or galvanized lines. We also check for secondary damage and can coordinate with restoration professionals if water damage has already occurred. Every leak detection and repair service comes backed by our price beat guarantee, so you can be confident you're getting the best value in the Temecula Valley.
