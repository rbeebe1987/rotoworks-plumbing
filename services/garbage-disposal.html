Fast Garbage Disposal Repair & Replacement

Your garbage disposal is one of the hardest-working appliances in your kitchen, grinding up food waste day after day. When it jams, leaks, or stops working altogether, it disrupts your entire kitchen routine and can lead to backed-up drains and unpleasant odors. RotoWorks Plumbing provides fast, affordable garbage disposal repair and replacement throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee. We service all major brands including InSinkErator, Waste King, Moen, and GE, and most repairs are completed in a single visit.

The most common disposal problems we encounter include a unit that hums but won't spin — typically caused by a foreign object lodged between the impellers and the grinding ring — a disposal that won't turn on at all due to a tripped reset button, blown fuse, or failed motor, persistent leaking from the sink flange, dishwasher connection, or bottom of the unit, and loud grinding or rattling noises that indicate loose mounting hardware or worn internal components. We also see frequent issues from homeowners putting fibrous foods like celery, artichoke leaves, or corn husks into the unit, which can wrap around the impellers and cause jams. Our technicians safely clear jams, replace worn components, and advise you on best practices to extend the life of your disposal.

When a repair isn't cost-effective — usually when the motor has burned out or the unit is leaking from the housing — we recommend replacement. We'll help you choose the right horsepower and features for your household size and usage, remove the old unit, install the new one, and test it thoroughly. Modern disposals operate more quietly, grind more efficiently, and include features like auto-reverse to reduce future jams. Every installation and repair comes with our price beat guarantee and upfront pricing.
