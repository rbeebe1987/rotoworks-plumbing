Is your water heater acting up?

When your water heater fails, your entire household feels it — no hot showers, no clean dishes, no laundry. RotoWorks Plumbing provides fast, reliable water heater repair and replacement for both traditional tank units and modern tankless systems throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee. We service all major brands including Rheem, A.O. Smith, Bradford White, Rinnai, and Navien, so no matter what's in your garage or utility closet, we can help.

Common signs that your water heater needs attention include inconsistent water temperature, rusty or discolored hot water, unusual popping or rumbling sounds from the tank, water pooling around the base, and a unit that's more than 10 years old. If your system is showing any of these warning signs, it's smart to call before a small issue turns into a flooded garage or a complete loss of hot water. Our technicians diagnose the problem on the spot and explain your options clearly — repair versus replacement — so you can make the best decision for your home and budget.

Considering an upgrade to tankless? Tankless water heaters provide endless hot water on demand, take up less space, and can cut your energy bills by up to 30 percent compared to a traditional tank. RotoWorks handles the full installation including gas line modifications, venting, and permitting. We also offer same-day tank water heater replacements when your old unit has reached the end of its life. Every installation comes with manufacturer warranty support and our price beat guarantee.
