When Disaster Strikes, RotoWorks Responds

Plumbing emergencies don't follow a schedule. A pipe can burst at midnight, a sewer line can back up on a holiday weekend, and a water heater can fail in the middle of winter. When these situations happen, every minute counts — water damage spreads fast, sewage poses serious health risks, and a gas leak can be genuinely dangerous. That's why RotoWorks Plumbing provides true 24/7 emergency plumbing service throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee. When you call us, a real plumber answers the phone, and we dispatch a fully equipped technician to your home as quickly as possible.

Our emergency services cover the full range of plumbing crises that homeowners face. Burst or frozen pipes that are actively flooding your home, main sewer line backups that send sewage into your bathtub or floor drains, complete loss of hot water during cold weather, water heater leaks that are flooding your garage or utility room, gas line leaks that you can smell in your home, overflowing toilets that won't stop running, and any situation where water is actively entering your living space and causing damage. We arrive with fully stocked trucks carrying the parts, tools, and equipment needed to stop the damage and make the repair on the spot — not just temporarily patch it until a weekday appointment.

We understand that emergencies are stressful, and the last thing you need is a surprise bill on top of the damage. That's why we provide upfront pricing even on emergency calls — before we start any work, you'll know exactly what it will cost. We don't charge inflated after-hours rates to take advantage of a bad situation. Our price beat guarantee applies to emergency service too, so if you've gotten a quote from another plumber, we'll beat it. When your home is in crisis, RotoWorks is the call that gets results.
