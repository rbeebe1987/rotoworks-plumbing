Reliable Water Line Repair & Repiping

Your water line is the lifeline of your home's plumbing system, carrying fresh water from the city main to every faucet, shower, and appliance in your house. When that line is damaged, corroded, or failing, the effects are immediate — low water pressure throughout the home, discolored or rusty water at the tap, unexplained wet areas in the yard, or a sudden spike in your water bill. RotoWorks Plumbing provides expert water line repair, replacement, and whole-home repiping throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee to restore your water supply quickly and affordably.

Many homes in the Temecula Valley built before the 1980s still have original galvanized steel water lines, which corrode from the inside out over time. As the corrosion builds up, it restricts water flow, causing low pressure and brownish water that's unappealing and can stain fixtures. Galvanized pipes can also develop pinhole leaks that go unnoticed for months, leading to water damage under slabs or in crawl spaces. If your home has galvanized piping, a whole-home repipe to modern copper or PEX tubing is often the smartest long-term investment. PEX is flexible, corrosion-resistant, and costs less to install than rigid copper, making it an excellent choice for Southern California homes.

For acute water line breaks — caused by shifting soil, tree root pressure, or accidental damage during excavation — we provide emergency repair service to stop the water loss and restore your supply as quickly as possible. We also handle water main connections, shut-off valve replacements, and pressure regulator installations to ensure consistent, safe water pressure throughout your home. Every water line service comes with a thorough inspection, transparent pricing, and our price beat guarantee so you know you're getting the best value available.
