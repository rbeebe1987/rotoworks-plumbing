Complete Faucet & Fixture Services for Your Home

A dripping faucet may not seem like a big deal, but even a slow drip can waste more than 3,000 gallons of water per year and add unnecessary cost to your utility bill. Beyond wasted water, worn-out fixtures can cause low pressure, inconsistent temperature, and unsightly mineral buildup that makes your kitchen or bathroom look dated. RotoWorks Plumbing handles every type of faucet and fixture repair and installation throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee — from a simple washer replacement to a full kitchen or bathroom faucet upgrade.

Our fixture services cover kitchen faucets, bathroom sink faucets, bathtub faucets, showerheads and shower valves, utility sink faucets, outdoor hose bibs, and shut-off valves throughout your home. Common problems we repair include persistent dripping caused by worn cartridges or O-rings, handles that are difficult to turn due to corrosion or mineral buildup, low water pressure from clogged aerators, and leaking connections under sinks. We work with all major brands including Moen, Delta, Kohler, Pfister, and American Standard, and we carry a wide selection of common replacement parts on our trucks for fast, single-visit repairs.

Planning a kitchen or bathroom remodel? RotoWorks installs new faucets, sinks, showerheads, and other fixtures to your exact specifications. We ensure proper connection to your existing supply lines, check for leaks, and verify water pressure and temperature before the job is done. Whether you're fixing a simple drip or upgrading every fixture in your home, you'll get transparent pricing and our price beat guarantee on every job.
