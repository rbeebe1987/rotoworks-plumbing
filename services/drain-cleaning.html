Professional Drain Cleaning You Can Count On

A clogged drain is more than an inconvenience — it can lead to foul odors, standing water, and even sewage backup if left untreated. Whether your kitchen sink is draining slowly, your shower is pooling water, or your main line is completely blocked, RotoWorks Plumbing delivers fast, thorough drain cleaning throughout Temecula, Murrieta, and Menifee. We arrive same-day in most cases, diagnose the problem quickly, and get your drains flowing freely again.

Our technicians use professional-grade equipment including motorized drain snakes and high-pressure hydro-jetting machines to break through even the toughest clogs. Grease buildup in kitchen lines, hair and soap scum in bathroom drains, tree root intrusion in main sewer lines — we've seen and cleared it all. Hydro-jetting is especially effective for recurring clogs because it scours the interior walls of your pipes, removing years of accumulated buildup rather than just punching a hole through the blockage.

We also perform camera inspections when the cause of a clog isn't immediately clear. A small waterproof camera is fed into the drain to reveal cracks, root intrusion, collapsed sections, or foreign objects that standard snaking can't address. This allows us to recommend the most cost-effective solution — whether that's a simple clearing, hydro-jetting, or a targeted repair. Every job comes with upfront pricing and our price beat guarantee, so you'll never pay more than you should.
